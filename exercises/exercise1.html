About Paige

Who I Am

Born and raised in Groton, MA, I am currently a sophomore at the Universtiy of Massachusetts Amherst studying English with a focus on creative writing.

Longer Bio

With my degree in English I want to be involved in the creation of stories that connect with people and build a better world. I have experience writing short stories and copy editing other people’s writing.

At UMass Amherst I am a part of the English Department’s Student Leadership Group and am specifically involved with the creation of the student-run literary magazine, The Scribe.
