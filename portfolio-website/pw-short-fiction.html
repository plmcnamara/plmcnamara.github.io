Short Fiction

The Decision

In a mysterious manor deep inside a dark forest forever trapped in an endless night, one by one children stumble through the doors looking for a safe place to stay. The cost for this safety, however, is a steep one, and for one little girl, the weight of her Decision weighs heavy on her shoulders until she grows to resent it.
This horror short story examines the lengths that people will go to for sense of safety and what happens when that sacrifice holds too high a cost.

Clara and Her Wolves

Clara is a young girl who spends her time deep in the woods with her best friend: a large mystical wolf named Snow. Despite this friendship, Clara still can't help but feel lonely, until one day another wolf appears and her small family grows that much larger.
This character-driven short story is a love letter to the importance of connection that people are able to form, no matter how unlikely they might seem.

The Silenced

From a young age, a girl is haunted by a mysterious apparition of a woman who can't speak. Unable to truly communicate with one another, their relationship becomes strained when the woman suddenly attacks a coworker after he dismisses the girl one too many times.
In this speculative fiction short story, the many ways in which women are silenced and silence themselves is explored through two woman who, no matter how much they try to speak, are never listened to.
