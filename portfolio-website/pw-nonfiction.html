Knowledge as a Path to Liberation (Academic Paper)

This academic paper examines how knowledge in Frederick Douglass’ memoir My Bondage and My Freedom is both withheld and used by Douglass while growing up enslaved, and how his quest to attain and share knowledge resulted in both his freedom and the freedom of others.
